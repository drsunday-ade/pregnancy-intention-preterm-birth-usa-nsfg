
NSFG 2022–2023 Female Pregnancy Public-Use Data File
---------------------------------------------------

This repository uses ONLY openly available, de-identified human data from the
National Survey of Family Growth (NSFG), produced and distributed by the
National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS), Centers for Disease Control and
Prevention (CDC).

Original data source (public-use files):
https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/nsfg/nsfg-2022-2023-puf.htm

The NSFG public-use data are freely available to the public without
registration. All data used in this project were downloaded from the NSFG
public-use data portal prior to initiation of the analyses. No new data were
collected for this study, and no attempt has been or will be made to
re-identify any survey participant.

Users of this repository who wish to work with the NSFG data should obtain the
most recent public-use files and documentation directly from the CDC/NCHS
website above, and must comply with all guidance and terms of use provided by
NCHS.

Suggested data citation (generic form):

National Center for Health Statistics. National Survey of Family Growth,
2022–2023 Public-Use Data Files. Hyattsville, MD: Centers for Disease Control
and Prevention, U.S. Department of Health and Human Services.
